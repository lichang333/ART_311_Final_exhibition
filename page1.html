Get Access

To get access to the USDA Food composition Dtabase API, you need obtain an API key account and register your own account at first. Just go to the USDA Nutrient database API doc page and search for "API key".






On the "Gaining Access" paragraph, click the link "Sign up Now" and put your personal information on it. And then click "Sign up".
